Requirements for Establishing a Regional
Headquarters (RHQ) in Saudi Arabia

The company is required to have operations in at least two countries, not including Saudi Arabia or the country where the headquarters is located.

The RHQ in Saudi Arabia must function as a distinct division or branch of the parent company.

It is compulsory to initiate operations within six months following the issuance of the RHQ License by MISA.

Obtain a verified copy of the last financial year's annual audited consolidated financial statements, certified by the Saudi Embassy.
